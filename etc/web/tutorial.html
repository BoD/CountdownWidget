What are widgets?

Widgets are mini apps that run directly on your home screen, and show "at-a-glance" information (for instance, weather, stock, etc.).
Some phones also allow to put them on your lock screen.
A more complete definition.

How to add a widget to the home screen

This depends on your particular brand / model, but they often have similar ways to do it.
Usually, you just long-tap on your home screen and choose "Widgets", and you'll see a list to choose from.
On some phones, you have to go to the app drawer, and you'll see a "Widgets" tab there.
